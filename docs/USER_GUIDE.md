## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. Navigate to: `https://yourdomain.com`
3. You will see the login page

### First Time Login

**For New Employees:**
1. Check your email for welcome message from HR
2. Note your Employee ID and default password (CRMSemp123!)
3. Navigate to the login page
4. Enter your Employee ID and default password
5. Click "Login"
6. You'll be prompted to change your password
7. Complete your profile with additional information

**For Existing Employees:**
1. Enter your Employee ID and password
2. Click "Login"
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

**Note:** Only HR personnel can register new employees in the system. Employees cannot self-register.

### Forgot Password

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Click the reset link and enter your new password

## HR Management

### Registering New Employees (HR Personnel Only)

1. Go to "HR Management" > "Employees"
2. Click "Add Employee" or "Register Employee"
3. Fill in required information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Department
   - Job Grade
   - Role
4. Click "Create Employee"
5. System generates Employee ID automatically
6. Welcome email sent to employee with:
   - Employee ID
   - Default password (CRMSemp123!)
   - Login instructions

**Important:** Only HR personnel have permission to register new employees.

### Viewing Employee Directory

1. Click "HR Management" in the sidebar
2. Select "Employees"
3. You'll see a list of all employees

**Search & Filter:**
- Use the search box to find employees by name or ID
- Filter by department, position, or status
- Click column headers to sort

**View Employee Details:**
- Click on any employee name
- View complete profile including:
  - Personal information
  - Employment details
  - Contact information
  - Emergency contacts
  - Documents

### Completing Your Profile (First Time Users)

**After first login with default password:**
1. System prompts you to change password
2. Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Confirm new password
4. Click "Update Password"
5. Complete your profile:
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Date of birth
   - Emergency contact details
   - Bank account information
   - Upload profile photo
6. Click "Save Profile"

### Managing Your Profile

1. Click your name in the top right corner
2. Select "My Profile"
3. View and update:
   - Contact information (phone, address)
   - Emergency contacts
   - Bank details
   - Profile photo

**Note:** Core employment details (name, email, department, position) can only be changed by HR.

### Viewing Payslips

1. Go to "HR Management" > "My Payslips"
2. Select the month/year
3. Click "View" to see payslip details
4. Click "Download PDF" to save a copy

**Payslip Information:**
- Basic salary
- Allowances
- Gross pay
- Deductions (tax, insurance, pension)
- Net pay

### Attendance

**Viewing Your Attendance:**
1. Go to "HR Management" > "My Attendance"
2. Select date range
3. View attendance records with:
   - Check-in time
   - Check-out time
   - Work hours
   - Status

**Biometric Check-in:**
1. Approach the biometric device
2. Place finger on scanner or look at camera
3. Wait for confirmation
4. System records your attendance

## Leave Management

### Checking Leave Balance

1. Go to "Leave Management" > "My Leave"
2. View your leave balances:
   - Annual leave
   - Sick leave
   - Emergency leave
   - Other leave types
3. See entitled, used, pending, and available days

### Submitting Leave Request

1. Go to "Leave Management" > "Request Leave"
2. Fill in the form:
   - Leave type
   - Start date
   - End date
   - Reason
   - Contact during leave
3. System shows days requested and remaining balance
4. Click "Submit Request"
5. You'll receive email notification when approved/rejected

**Tips:**
- Submit requests at least 3 days in advance
- Check team calendar for conflicts
- Ensure sufficient leave balance
- Provide detailed reason for emergency leave

### Viewing Leave History

1. Go to "Leave Management" > "Leave History"
2. View all your leave requests with:
   - Dates
   - Type
   - Status (pending, approved, rejected)
   - Approver comments
3. Filter by status or date range

### Approving Leave (For Managers)

1. Go to "Leave Management" > "Team Leave Requests"
2. View pending requests from your team
3. Click on a request to see details
4. Review:
   - Employee leave balance
   - Team calendar
   - Reason for leave
5. Click "Approve" or "Reject"
6. Add comments if needed
7. Click "Confirm"

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get access to the system?**
A: Contact HR to register you as an employee. You'll receive an email with your Employee ID and default password.

**Q: Can I register myself in the system?**
A: No. Only HR personnel can register new employees. Contact your HR department for account creation.

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and you'll receive a password reset with the default password (CRMSemp123!). Change it immediately after login.

**Q: What is my username for login?**
A: Your username is your Employee ID (e.g., EMP001), not your email address.

**Q: How do I update my contact information?**
A: Go to your profile and update phone, address, and emergency contacts. Core employment details can only be changed by HR.

**Q: I'm not receiving email notifications. What's wrong?**
A: Check your notification settings and ensure your email address is correct in your profile. Also check your spam folder.

### Leave Management

**Q: How much notice do I need to give for leave?**
A: Generally, 3 days advance notice is required. Emergency leave can be requested with less notice.

**Q: Can I cancel an approved leave request?**
A: Yes, go to your leave history and click "Cancel" on the approved request. Your manager will be notified.

**Q: What happens to unused leave at year-end?**
A: Depending on leave type and company policy, unused leave may be carried forward or forfeited.

### Procurement

**Q: How long does it take to approve a purchase request?**
A: Typically 3-5 business days, depending on the amount and approval hierarchy.

**Q: Can I track my purchase request?**
A: Yes, go to "My Requests" to see the current status and any comments from approvers.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The system is slow. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. Contact IT if the issue persists.

**Q: I can't access a certain module. Why?**
A: You may not have permission to access that module. Contact your manager or IT administrator.

**Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?**
A: Go to "Help" > "Support Ticket" and submit your request.
